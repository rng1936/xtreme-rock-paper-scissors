Rules

  1. Each player starts with 15 weapons: 5 rock, 5 paper and 5 scissors. Your objective is to empty your opponent's arsenal.

  2. For each round, choose a weapon from your arsenal.

  3. The winner of a round will gain the weapon that the loser chose that round.

  4. After a round, click Next Round to continue the game.

  5. If the round was tied, both players recover the weapon they chose that round.

  6. If three consecutive rounds are tied, one random weapon is removed from you and your opponent's arsenal.

  7. If a player has ran out of two weapons, that player loses the game.

  8. If you use the same weapon three times in a row, that weapon will break and cannot be used for the remainder of the game.

  9. Click Reset Game if you feel that the computer is beating you or to play again.
